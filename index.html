What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple calculation using your height and weight. It's a useful screening tool to indicate whether you're in a healthy weight range.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obese: ≥ 30

* Consult with our trainers for a comprehensive body composition analysis.
